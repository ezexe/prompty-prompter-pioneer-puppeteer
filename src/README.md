## 🏗️ Architecture

The prompt is the whole of it — the starting point, the route, and the destination. Not source that compiles into a frozen artifact you then run; the journey itself, carried in language. You don't ship a finished pathway and make the engine walk yours.

## 🤝 Contract

Instruct the engine through a prompt. Only what the prompt carries can be computed — the starting point, route, and destination live in the intent, or there is nothing to perceive and no response to give. The true intent is in the words of the prompt; the engine holds the perception it computes of that intent. The contract is the shared perception they agree on.

## 🧰 Plugins

MCP servers, plugins, Cowork, Claude Desktop — quirky gizmos. Too many interfaces adding too much fluff. The engine takes action not because the wiring inferred it, but because it's sure of what the prompt intended.
